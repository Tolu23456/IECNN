## 🧩 The biggest missing formula in IECNN

# 🔥 Emergent Utility Gradient (EUG)

---

## 🧠 The missing formula

### 🔹 Emergent Utility Gradient

U(p_t) = \mathbb{E}[C_{t+1}(p)] - C_t(p)

---

### What this does:

It measures:

> “Will this prediction become more useful in the next iteration or not?”

---

## ⚡ Why you need this

Right now your system stops because:

* novelty drops
* entropy stabilizes

But it does NOT know:

> “Am I getting closer to a better structure, or just repeating myself?”

---

## 🧠 What this fixes in your logs

With EUG added:

### Instead of:

* low_novelty_gain → stop

### You get:

* “keep going, structure is still improving”

* “stop, no future gain expected”
